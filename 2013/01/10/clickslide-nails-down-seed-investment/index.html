ClickSlide Nails down seed investment

January 10, 2013 Gabriel OrtizComments Off

ClickSlide Ltd. announces the successful closure of its seed round.  The investment, which will be used to fund product development, has come from the Ariadne Capital Entrepreneurs (ACE) Fund and three angel investors.

ClickSlide Abacus is a cloud-based platform that allows software to be connected to a Programmable interface for the testing of APIs against live mobile applications.  Such technology can create a substantial reduction in application build time. This technology also promises to give people who couldn’t normally contemplate it, the opportunity to take part in application development.

ClickSlide Abacus can produce a live application connected to any REST, SOAP and XML -RPC APIs. Live testing of APIs against mobile applications can be used to test API viability at hack-a-thons. ClickSlide Abacus is used to educate developers on how an API can be utilized within a live application. Due to the huge divide between modern and legacy systems, API testing and implementation is a challenge. Allowing developers to simply connect any API to a GUI results in a significant reduction of development cost.

The Company intends to launch ClickSlide Abacus this year.  It’s a UI for connecting any API data to a flat-pack mobile app without using code.  ClickSlide Abacus is the Company’s flat-pack software solution for enterprises that want to benefit both customers and employees by taking advantage of the vast amount of data at their disposal.

Announcing the investment, Gabriel Ortiz, Chief Executive Officer of ClickSlide (pictured above), said:

“Aaron Franco and I founded ClickSlide because we believe that the world’s technology needs to acquire an added dimension of humanity.  Too often technology excludes rather than includes and our belief is that there should be fewer barriers to its creation.  The greater the participation in the development of technology, the less we fragment not just software and applications, but society as a whole.  That’s our vision and we want to work with companies that share that vision.”

Julie Meyer, Managing Partner, Ariadne Capital Entrepreneurs (ACE) Fund, said:

“When we first met the ClickSlide team we were immediately excited by what they were working on.  We believe that they’re doing exactly what entrepreneurs should always do – set out to change the world. The technology that Clickslide is developing promises to do just that.  It’s the ability to break the mould, to do things differently, that leads to true progress.  We think ClickSlide is a game-changer.”

Aaron Franco, Chief Technology Officer of ClickSlide said:

“We’re really pleased that Ariadne Capital decided to invest in ClickSlide. They understood immediately what we were trying to do and it was quite clear to us that they really wanted to get involved.  We believe that their experience of the international market place will help us to establish ClickSlide as a global brand much more quickly.”
